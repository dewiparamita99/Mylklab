Mylk Lab is an emerging brand focused on plant-based food and beverages. Our logo symbolize a healthy heartbeat of a human. Which we hope could patronize a movement of investing in healthy living and being, through a message we deliver to you.

In Mylk Lab, we aim to deliver healthy, organic, and pure products for our customer. We extremely proud to present "fresh" and "locally handmade" products using excellent raw quality produces without preservatives, additives, fillers, carrageans, and many other unnecessary ingredients.
All our prodcuts are thoughtfully designed and packed in sustainable packaging possible.
